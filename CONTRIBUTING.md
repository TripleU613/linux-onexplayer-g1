# Contributing

Got the G1 (or a sibling handheld) working better on Linux? Please check in your fix.

- **Open an issue** to report what works / doesn't, even without a fix.
- **Open a PR** to add or improve a fix. Keep it as a drop-in file (config / udev rule / small script) plus a couple lines in the README.

When reporting, include:
- G1 variant + CPU (e.g. Ryzen AI 9 HX 370), and distro + kernel (`uname -r`).
- The device IDs involved (`lsusb`, `/sys/.../name`).
- What you changed and how you confirmed it works.

That's it — small, verified fixes welcome.
